Meta-Wrappers
=============

Meta-wrappers offer curated and tested combinations of :ref:`wrappers` that fulfil common tasks with popular tools, in a best-practice way.
For using them, simply copy-paste the offered snippets into your `Snakemake <https://snakemake.github.io>`_ workflow.

The menu on the left (expand by clicking (+) if necessary), lists all available meta-wrappers.
